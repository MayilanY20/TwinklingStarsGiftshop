About Us

Welcome to Twinkling Stars Giftshop — a labour of love born from the belief that every gift should feel like a warm embrace. We are a passionate small team of artisans who handcraft each piece with care, creativity, and an eye for elegance.

From our fragrant flower bouquets to our delicate charm jewellery and personalised photo frames, everything is made to order — because your moments deserve something truly one-of-a-kind.
